Crystal Collector!

Instructions

At the start the of the game, the player will be shown a random number at the bottom the screen. As the player clicks on the crystals, the total score will increase by a specific amount. To win, the player must match their total score to the random number from the beginning of the game If the player's total score goes above the random number, you lose. The game automatically restarts after player wins or loses.
